ABOUT US

One engine for omnichannel performance on Meta.

We build the backbone that performance teams, agencies, and creator-led brands need to ship cross-channel conversion campaigns in minutes, not weeks.

Why we exist

Modern advertisers are split across web, app, and offline events. Meta's Cross-Channel Conversion Optimization unifies them with a single optimisation event — but actually building, migrating, and measuring those campaigns at scale is still a wall of API plumbing. We built this engine so teams can stop fighting the plumbing and start moving budget intelligently.

What we believe

  • Decisions over dashboards. Reports are only useful when they suggest the next move. Our analytics surface deltas you can act on — channel mix, predicted CPA, the next budget reallocation.
  • Privacy is non-negotiable. Encryption at rest, per-permission justification, transparent retention, real data subject rights. We treat your data the way we'd want ours treated.
  • The plumbing should be boring. OAuth, omnichannel link specs, tracking specs, migration plans — all of it should "just work" so your team can focus on creative and strategy.
  • Independent and aligned. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by Meta Platforms, Inc. We build on top of Meta's published Marketing API and ship to the same standards Meta sets for its developer ecosystem.

Who it's for

  • Performance teams running Sales/Lead/App-install objectives across web and app surfaces.
  • Agencies managing multiple advertiser accounts who need consolidated CCCO workflows.
  • Creator and influencer-led brands shipping campaigns alongside content drops.
  • Engineering teams who want a clean, audited Marketing API surface instead of writing their own.
