2. Personal data we collect

We collect only the data needed to provide the Service. Specifically:

  • Account & authentication data: Meta user ID, name, email (from Meta Business Login), ad account IDs you authorise.
  • OAuth tokens: long-lived Meta access tokens, stored encrypted at rest.
  • Campaign configuration: ad set names, budgets, omnichannel objects, pixel/app IDs, web URLs, deep links, tracking specs that you supply.
  • Insights: aggregate ad-set performance data (conversions, spend, CPA, channel split) pulled from the Meta Marketing API on your instruction.
  • Billing data: Stripe customer ID, subscription tier, invoice references. Card data is held by Stripe; we never receive or store it.
  • Technical data: request logs, timestamps, IP, user agent — retained only as long as necessary for security and abuse prevention.
  • Privacy & rights requests: name, email, request type, free-text description you submit through the DSR form.

7. Your rights

Under the EU/UK GDPR you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. Exercise any of these via our DSR submission form or by emailing privacy@[YOUR_DOMAIN]. We respond within one calendar month, free of charge, in line with Article 12(3).

If you are a California resident, equivalent rights under the CCPA/CPRA — including the right to know, delete, correct, and limit the use of sensitive personal information — are honoured through the same channels.

8. Meta-specific deletion

Per Meta's developer platform requirements, you may also trigger deletion of data we hold about your Meta account by removing our app from your Meta Business Settings. Meta will then notify us with a signed deletion request, and we will purge your session tokens and stage your downstream records for deletion. You will receive a confirmation code you can use to check the status of the deletion.

9. Security

We apply technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk, including: encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), encryption at rest for OAuth tokens, least-privilege access controls, audit logging, vulnerability management, and routine penetration testing. No system is perfectly secure; if a breach occurs that risks your rights or freedoms we will notify you and the relevant authority within 72 hours per Art. 33.

10. Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 18 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it.
